🚨 Function Call 测试用例

测试说明： 双击下面的单词，然后单击激活翻译。这些单词应该触发DeepSeek的alert提醒功能。

1. 冒犯性/敏感词汇测试

⚠️ 这些词汇可能包含冒犯内容，仅用于测试敏感词检测功能

The medical term retarded is now considered offensive.

Using the word negro is inappropriate in modern context.

The term mongoloid is an outdated and offensive classification.

Calling someone crippled is considered insensitive.

The word midget is offensive to people with dwarfism.

2. 极其罕见/专业术语测试

📚 这些是极其少见或高度专业化的词汇

The defenestration of Prague was a historical event.

She suffered from pneumonoultramicroscopicsilicovolcanoconiosiss, a lung disease.

The floccinaucinihilipilification of his efforts was unfair.

Ancient hippopotomonstrosesquippedaliophobia affects many people.

The scholar studied antidisestablishmentarianism movements.

3. 文化敏感性词汇测试

🌍 这些词汇在不同文化背景下可能有敏感含义

The term oriental is considered outdated when referring to people.

Using gypsy can be offensive to Romani people.

The word tribe can be problematic in certain contexts.

Saying primitive about cultures is culturally insensitive.

The term savage has colonial and racist connotations.

4. 古英语/方言词汇测试

📜 这些是古英语或地方方言词汇

The old man said forsooth in agreement.

She wore a beautiful kirtle to the medieval fair.

The knight's hauberk was made of chainmail.

He spoke in prithee to the lord.

The varlet served in the castle.

5. 极度专业术语测试

🔬 这些是高度专业化的学术术语

The patient showed signs of pseudohypoparathyroidism.

The chemical tetraiodophenolphthalein was used in the experiment.

She studied otorhinolaryngology in medical school.

The electroencephalography results were inconclusive.

The magnetohydrodynamics theory is complex.

6. 普通词汇对照组

✅ 这些是普通词汇，不应该触发alert

The spring season is beautiful.

This is a legal document.

Here's an example of good writing.

The computer is working fine.

She has a beautiful voice.
