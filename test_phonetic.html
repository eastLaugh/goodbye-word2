🔤 音标显示功能测试

测试说明： 双击下面的单词，然后单击激活翻译。翻译完成后，音标应该会显示在单词上方，持续3秒后自动消失。

常用单词测试

These are common words that should have clear phonetic transcriptions:

The spring season brings beautiful flowers.

This is a legal document for your review.

Here's a good example of proper usage.

The computer is running smoothly.

She has a beautiful singing voice.

复杂单词测试

These words have more complex phonetic transcriptions:

The pronunciation guide is helpful.

This phenomenon is quite interesting.

The architecture is magnificent.

We need to communicate effectively.

The restaurant serves excellent food.

音标显示要求

  • ✅ 音标应该显示在选中单词的正上方
  • ✅ 使用淡淡的颜色，不干扰阅读
  • ✅ 字体较小，使用等宽字体
  • ✅ 有淡入淡出动画效果
  • ✅ 3秒后自动消失
  • ✅ 不影响原页面布局
